ADHD has three categories.

3 types of ADHD

  1. Hyperactive
  2. Inattentive
  3. Combination

But now with God's mercy I am able to control my hyperactiveness but one still remains... MY INATTENTIVENESS!. Having ADHD is not the absent of focus but rather trouble maintaining focus and our Executive Function are poorly regulated. ADHD brains are low on dopamine or happy hormones so we are in search for activities that gave us dopamine, so it's hard for me to do task that I don't have the interest in no matter how much focused we give in the end it does'nt matter, this results to late submissions, low grades, overwhelmed.

To a neurotyoical person this may look like I procrastinating, but in the truth I'm not. I saying this not to justify my shortcomings but to explain, to inform other people about the effects of having ADHD.
